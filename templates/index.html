Our web application consists of sixteen neural networks,
which are pre-trained using data from over 750 000 homes
across England and Wales. The neural networks are tested
against data from 60 000 different homes.


Other carbon calculators require users to input their
electricity and gas usage (or other fuels), whilst ours uses
features of a property, making it useful for home seekers.
We also provide potential values and the estimated cost of
lighting, and heating and hot water.


Training and testing data sourced from Domestic Energy
Performance Certificates published by the Ministry of Housing,
Communities and Local Government, containing public sector
information licensed under the Open Government Licence v3.0.
Data entered by users is not collected nor stored.
